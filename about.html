About Us

Fog Catching Nets

A fog fence, fog collector or fog net are all different names for the same technique. This technique creates an alternative source of fresh water through harvesting water from fog, using large pieces of vertical mesh netting to induce the fog-droplets to flow down towards a channel below.

We aim to utilize this technique to make contributions toward a more sustainable world through reducing waste from fashion industry by recycling it into fog catching nets.By donating us, not only are you recieving monetarily benefits, but you're also helping a greater cause and becoming a part of the change.
